# Trust Africa User Flow

## Main Button

Start a Trusted Agreement

---

## Step 1: Welcome

Trust Africa welcomes the user with one message:

No honest person should ever have to choose between opportunity and safety.

---

## Step 2: Trust Guide

The Trust Guide asks:

What are you trying to achieve together?

---

## Step 3: Agreement Details

The user provides:

- Buyer name
- Seller name
- Product or service
- Amount
- Delivery date
- Required evidence

---

## Step 4: Hidden Concern Check

The Trust Guide asks:

Is there anything that would make you feel unsafe about this trade that we have not discussed?

---

## Step 5: AI Agreement Review

The AI checks:

- Is the agreement clear?
- Is the amount defined?
- Is the delivery date clear?
- Is the evidence requirement strong enough?

If unclear, it asks for clarification.

---

## Step 6: Escrow

After both parties accept, the buyer deposits USDC into escrow.

---

## Step 7: Evidence Upload

Seller uploads:

- Receipt
- Tracking number
- Photos

Buyer can upload:

- Delivery confirmation
- Complaint evidence
- Product photos

---

## Step 8: AI Review

AI asks:

- What was promised?
- What happened?
- What evidence exists?
- How confident am I?

---

## Step 9: Decision

If confidence is high:

Release or refund funds.

If confidence is low:

Send to human validators.

---

## Step 10: Trust Grows

After a successful agreement, both parties build reputation.

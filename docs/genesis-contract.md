# Trust Africa Genesis Contract

## Mission

Protect one honest agreement between two strangers.

---

## Contract Lifecycle

### Stage 1

Create Agreement

Store:

- Buyer
- Seller
- Product
- Amount
- Delivery Date
- Evidence Requirements

---

### Stage 2

AI Agreement Validation

Check:

- Is the agreement clear?
- Is the amount defined?
- Is the delivery date defined?
- Is evidence sufficient?

---

### Stage 3

Escrow Funding

Buyer deposits USDC.

Funds are protected.

---

### Stage 4

Seller Performance

Seller delivers the goods or service.

---

### Stage 5

Evidence Collection

Collect:

- Tracking Number
- Receipt
- Photos
- Delivery Confirmation

---

### Stage 6

AI Reasoning

The AI asks:

- What was promised?
- What happened?
- What evidence exists?
- How certain am I?

---

### Stage 7

Decision

If confidence is high:

Release funds.

If confidence is low:

Request human validators.

---

## Trust Africa Principles

1. No honest person should ever have to choose between opportunity and safety.

2. Evidence is stronger than opinion.

3. AI must never pretend certainty.

4. Human wisdom protects uncertainty.

5. Trust grows through honest cooperation.

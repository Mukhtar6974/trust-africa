# Trust Africa

## Problem

Cross-border commerce across Africa often depends on counterparties that have never worked together. Evidence is fragmented, business reputation is difficult to verify, escrow decisions are slow, and dispute resolution can be expensive or inaccessible.

## Solution

Trust Africa is intelligent trust infrastructure for African commerce. It combines evidence-aware trade adjudication, autonomous escrow decisions, portable business reputation, and structured dispute resolution in one professional commerce workflow.

## Key Features

- **AI Trade Judge:** evaluates buyer, seller, product, value, and evidence to approve, reject, or route a trade for review.
- **Dynamic Trust Passports:** track trust score, completed trades, successful deliveries, dispute outcomes, and verification status.
- **Autonomous Escrow:** releases, refunds, or holds funds according to the final trust decision.
- **Evidence-Based Dispute Resolution:** compares buyer claims, seller responses, and supporting evidence.
- **Cross-Border Trade Intelligence:** presents protected commercial activity across African trade corridors.
- **Professional SaaS Experience:** provides focused views for trades, passports, AI decisions, escrow, disputes, and analytics.

## GenLayer Alignment

Trust Africa models its core workflow as a GenLayer intelligent contract. Commercial evidence is evaluated through autonomous reasoning rather than rigid keyword-only settlement rules. The contract exposes trade creation, evidence validation, dispute resolution, passport issuance, reputation updates, and full trust reporting. Explainable AI decisions coordinate certificate status, escrow state, and long-term business reputation.

## Demo Workflow

```text
Create Protected Trade
        → Submit Evidence
        → AI Trade Judge
        → Trust Certificate
        → Escrow Release / Refund / Hold
        → Trust Passport Update
```
